Here’s what happens after you search for something.

This is the search engine most people use because many believe that it is the only search engine in existence. It has a very simple and clean look making it very easy for even an 80 year old grandma to use. Google is the gold standard of search engines. When you search for something, you can get all kinds of results ranging from websites to videos. If you don’t manage to find what you are looking for in the first couple of pages of results, don’t worry. Google manages to provide you with an immense amount of results that may or may not drift further and further away from what you actually typed.

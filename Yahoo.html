Welcome to the search engine of 1996 baby! If you want a search engine that hasn’t really changed anything besides its presentation since you were born, then Yahoo is your go to. It has an informative presentation, showing news, weather, sports stats and videos, but having this many things presented to you at once can get a little overwhelming. The search function does provide useful results, but does not dive as deep, or provide as many options as some top tier search engines (Google). Hopefully, when you are done using this website they won’t get hacked again and lose all of your personal information.

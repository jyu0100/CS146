Changing your Default Search Engine for Dummies

Chrome

  1. In the search bar type in: chrome://settings
  2. In the settings search bar type in: search engine
  3. Select desired search engine from search engine used in address bar drop down list.

Firefox

  1. Navigate to your browser setting page.
  2. From the main settings page, enter the search settings tab on the left side.
  3. In the middle of the page, you will see a drop down menu where you can set your default search engine to your desired preference.

Edge

  1. Click on the settings button in the top right of the browser window, with 3 dots on it.
  2. Scroll down in the settings menu to find the advanced settings button
  3. In the middle of the advanced settings menu, you will find the option to select and change your default search engine to the engine of your choice.
